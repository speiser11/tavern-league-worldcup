THE DRAFT
8 players, 6 rounds, 48 teams total. Round 1 everyone picks a Tier A team. Rounds 2–6 are a snake draft — order reverses each round so the last pick in Round 2 goes first in Round 3, and so on. Each player ends up with 1 Tier A and 5 Tier B teams.
TIEBREAKERS
Ties broken by total wins. If still tied, the participant whose highest-seeded team advanced furthest wins. If still tied: best Tier B win pct. After that, it's a shot.
SIDE BETS
Wagers logged on this site, settled off-platform. They don't touch the main standings. Honor system on stakes (beer, $20, dishes, dares).
